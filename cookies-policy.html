What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit websites. They serve various functions, including enhancing browsing experiences, personalizing content and advertisements, providing social media features, and analyzing website traffic.
How We Utilize Cookies
We employ cookies for various purposes:

  • Authentication:
    Cookies authenticate and verify users during website logins.
  • Preferences:
    Cookies help us remember user preferences and settings.
  • Analytics:
    Cookies assist us in analyzing user interactions with our platform, enabling us to improve our services and content.
  • Advertising:
    Cookies enable us to deliver personalized ads to users based on their interests and browsing patterns.
Types of Cookies
Our platform utilizes both session and persistent cookies. Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain on your device for an extended period.
Cookie Management
There are several methods available for managing cookies:
  • Browser Settings:
    You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • Third-Party Tools:
    Opting out of specific third-party cookies can be done through online tools provided by organizations such as the Digital Advertising Alliance.
